## ARCHITECTURAL HIGHLIGHTS

### 1. Slide-to-Verify & Mandatory Consent Shield
Before accessing any task database, the application establishes a dual onboarding security container:

* **18+ Academic Legal Disclaimer**: A mandatory consent checkbox verifies adulthood and enforces the legal waiver. Users choosing to disagree are instantly redirected to a secure Google Search directory to ensure clean exit behavior.

### 2. Multi-Touch Parallax & Dynamic Light-Reflection Gloss
* **Visual Card Deck Stack**: The destiny card is layered over two background offset mock cards, constructing a physical playing deck with realistic drop shadows. When the top card is drawn, underlay cards wobble dynamically to mimic real paper traction.
* **Coordinate Gloss Shine**: Moving a cursor or sliding fingers on screens rotates the card on X/Y 3D axes and dynamically calculates `--shine-x` and `--shine-y` variables. A brilliant radial specular highlight follows touch movements, reproducing premium holographic gold cards.
* **Canvas Sparkle Emitter**: Dragging fingers or cursor moving emits high-velocity glowing cyan or pink stardust chimes that curve down under realistic gravity parameters.

5. Click the **Save** button to compile.
